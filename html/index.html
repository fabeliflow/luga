what are
dental

Dental implants are the gold standard in replacing missing teeth. Consisting only of three simple pieces, an implant is designed to replicate the function and appearance of your missing teeth. The titanium post is inserted into the bone, delivering a solid anchor for the connecting abutment and final dental crown. "A permanent restoration for a patient who can rely and depend and count on that tooth being healthy, strong and stable for a lifetime."The crown is shaped and shaded to match the surrounding teeth. One individual dental implant will make an ideal replacement for a lost tooth. The implant post, over time, integrates with existing bone, becoming a lasting, durable restoration.

Dental implants are meant to last a lifetime so it is important to have doctors experienced with advanced technology to ensure your success. Dr. Duffy uses 3D CAD/CAM software to carefully plan and place your implants in locations that will maximize bone density and avoid nerves and sinuses. Computer guided implant surgery offers unparalleled accuracy.

There is an option that combines the affordability of dentures with the stability of dental implants; they are implant supported dentures. With the addition of three or more implants, Dr. Duffy can retrofit your dentures so that they may be anchored to dental implants. This allows you to enjoy the stability and bone health properties of implants even if you are not a good candidate for traditional implants. We offer two methods: ball-retained and ball-retained. Both are removable yet durable.
